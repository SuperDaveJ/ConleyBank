RISK INDICATORS

Poor Internal Controls

Loan officer creating and approving GL tickets

Inadequate review and reconciliation of suspense items

Insider Abuse

A bank insider living outside of their means or showing other indications of financial stress

Large cash deposits by an insider

Transactions between a loan officer and a customer

Manipulating reports to conceal overdrafts from the Board of Directors

FRAUD TECHNIQUES

Follow the Money

Pulling Demand Deposit Account Statements (with front and back of checks and deposit slips) for unusual cash transactions

Pulling the GL Activity report to trace the flow of money for suspicious transactions

Pulling GL tickets and the block listing to verify the flow of money between accounts
